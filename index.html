Additional Rules

Construction Rules

  • Cars must hold together for the duration of the race without falling apart. Judges can disqualify any car that breaks apart mid-race.
  • The driver must have clear forward and side visibility for safe racing.
  • No glass, sharp metals, or other dangerous materials allowed.

Race Conduct Rules

  • Drivers must race their own cars without pushing or interfering with others.
  • You may block, but you risk crashing or losing positions. Block at your own risk.
  • All decisions regarding rules, collisions, or penalties by race officials are final.
  • No outside help or electronics allowed during the race
  • Designated Repair Time: Teams will have a set period for repairs between rounds.

Technical Rules

  • Maximum wheel diameter of 30 cm to keep races fair.
  • No motors, batteries, or electrical assistance allowed.
  • Maximum weight of the car (including driver) cannot exceed 100 kg.
